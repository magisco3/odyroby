Our Services

Companionship and Personal Support

We offer companionship and personal support to those who need it. Companion care can be individually tailored to meet a client’s unique needs. It often involves emotional support services, but it can also include a wide range of additional different non-medical services.

Day-to-Day Help

We offer help to seniors, adults with medical needs, and special needs children who require assistance with activities of daily living (ADLs) and instrumental activities of daily living (IADLs). Once again, our services are fully personalized: they can include everything from bathing & hygiene or medication setup & reminders to meal planning & preparation or transportation to appointments. Whatever in-home assistance is required can be made available.

Respite Care

Through our top-rated respite care services, we are able to offer short-term/time-limited relief to family members and unpaid caregivers who need some assistance. We can provide respite care on your schedule.

Specialized Care

Our highly trained home health care professionals have extensive experience working with patients in need of intensive, specialized assistance. Some notable examples of patients we have worked with include those in need of dementia care, Alzheimer’s care, traumatic brain injury (TBI) care, spinal cord injury care, and much more.

Special Needs & Behavioral Support

Our personal care professionals have the skills and compassion required to assist those with special needs and those who need behavioral support. If you are a parent or a caretaker of a vulnerable individual, we can help to set you up with the personal care services that are best suited for your unique situation.

About

dyroby serves those needing health care in their homes. Our staff will visit patients at home and provide the care needed to make it possible for them to stay home. We also understand that every situation is unique and requires different types of care. Our experienced staff will develop an individualized plan of care for each patient. Odyroby will do everything possible to make sure the schedule you want is the schedule you get.


We strive to positively impact the lives of our clients by providing excellent care and timely instruction that allows clients to make informed decisions and remain independent in their home.
