About Us

About Odyroby

Odyroby serves those needing health care in their homes. Our staff will visit patients at home and provide the care needed to make it possible for them to stay home. We also understand that every situation is unique and requires different types of care. Our experienced staff will develop an individualized plan of care for each patient. Odyroby will do everything possible to make sure the schedule you want is the schedule you get. Not only do we offer skilled nursing services but also offer a wide range of other skilled care and non-medical types of care. Our services are available seven days a week and on holidays.

Our Mission

The Mission of Personal Health Care Services is to provide safe, high quality care in the home that is cost effective and tailored to each patient's individual health and personal needs. Our goal is to assist our clients in maintaining their independence in the comfort of their own home, which has been proven to be the optimal healing
